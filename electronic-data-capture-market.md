<p><p>Electronic Data Capture (EDC) market is categorized into three main types: Web-hosted, Licensed Enterprise, and Cloud-based. Web-hosted EDC systems are accessed through a web browser, making them easy to use and deploy. Licensed Enterprise EDC systems are installed on-premises and offer more control over data security and customization. Cloud-based EDC systems are hosted on remote servers, providing scalability and flexibility. Each type offers distinct advantages based on the organization's needs, such as cost-effectiveness, data security, and accessibility.</p></p>
