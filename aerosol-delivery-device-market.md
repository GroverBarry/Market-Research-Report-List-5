<p><p>The aerosol delivery device market includes dry powder inhalers, metered dose inhalers, and nebulizers. Dry powder inhalers deliver medication in powder form, which is then inhaled into the lungs. Metered dose inhalers release a specific dose of medication in aerosol form, which is also inhaled. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into a fine mist for inhalation. Each type of device has its own advantages and is used for different conditions, providing options for patients with respiratory issues.</p></p>
