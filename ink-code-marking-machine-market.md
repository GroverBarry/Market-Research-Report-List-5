<p><p>Ink Code Marking Machines are available in two types: Contact and Non-Contact. Contact Ink Code Marking Machines physically touch the surface of the material to apply the code, while Non-Contact Ink Code Marking Machines do not require direct contact with the material, making them suitable for delicate or uneven surfaces. Both types of machines are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, packaging, and labeling to imprint codes, dates, logos, and other information on products for identification and tracking purposes.</p></p>
