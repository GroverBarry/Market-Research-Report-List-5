<p><p>Electric steam valves are commonly classified into two types: single acting steam valves and double acting steam valves. Single acting steam valves operate using one actuator to control the flow of steam, allowing it to open or close. On the other hand, double acting steam valves use two actuators to control the valve, providing more precise control over the flow of steam. Both types play a crucial role in regulating steam flow in various industrial applications.</p></p>
