<p><p>POS terminals in the retail market are divided into two main types: EMV POS terminals and Non-EMV POS terminals. EMV POS terminals are equipped with technology that can read and process EMV chip cards, providing enhanced security for transactions. On the other hand, Non-EMV POS terminals rely on magnetic stripe card technology, which is considered less secure. While EMV terminals are becoming the industry standard due to their increased security features, Non-EMV terminals are still commonly used in certain retail settings.</p></p>
