<p><p>Robotic palletizers and de-palletizers come in two main market types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic. Fully-automatic systems require minimal human intervention as they are programmed to handle the entire palletizing or de-palletizing process independently. On the other hand, semi-automatic systems require some level of human involvement, such as loading or unloading products onto the pallet. Both types of systems offer efficiency and productivity benefits, with fully-automatic systems being more suitable for high-volume operations and semi-automatic systems for smaller-scale applications.</p></p>
