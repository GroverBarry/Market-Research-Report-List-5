<p><p>InGaAs Image Sensors market can be classified into two main types: InGaAs Linear Image Sensors and InGaAs Area Image Sensors. InGaAs Linear Image Sensors are designed to capture images in a straight line, making them ideal for applications such as spectroscopy and industrial inspection. InGaAs Area Image Sensors, on the other hand, can capture images over a larger area, making them suitable for applications such as machine vision and surveillance. Both types of sensors utilize indium gallium arsenide technology to provide high sensitivity and performance in low-light conditions.</p></p>
