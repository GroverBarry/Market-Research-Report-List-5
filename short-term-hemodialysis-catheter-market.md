<p><p>Short-term hemodialysis catheters come in several types, including step-tip catheters, split-tip catheters, symmetric catheters, and others. Step-tip catheters feature a gradual transition from the shaft to the tip, while split-tip catheters have two separate tips for improved flow. Symmetric catheters have a symmetrical design for balanced flow rates. Other types of short-term hemodialysis catheters may include tunneled catheters, spiral catheters, or antimicrobial-coated catheters. Each type offers unique benefits and may be selected based on individual patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
