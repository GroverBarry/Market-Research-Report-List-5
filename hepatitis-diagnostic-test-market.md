<p><p>Hepatitis diagnostic tests include blood tests, imaging tests, and liver biopsies. Blood tests detect specific antibodies or antigens in the blood that indicate the presence of hepatitis viruses. Imaging tests, such as ultrasounds or MRIs, can show the condition of the liver and any abnormalities. Liver biopsies involve taking a small sample of liver tissue to examine for signs of inflammation, scarring, or other liver diseases. These different types of tests are crucial in diagnosing and monitoring hepatitis infections.</p></p>
