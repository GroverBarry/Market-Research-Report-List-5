<p><p>Consumer IAM market consists of various types of authentication methods to ensure secure access to consumer data. Passwords, knowledge-based answers, tokens, biometrics, PIN, and security certificates are popular methods used to authenticate consumers. Passwords require users to enter a specific combination of characters, knowledge-based answers require answering personal questions, tokens generate one-time codes for authentication, biometrics analyze unique biological characteristics, PIN is a personal identification number, and security certificates validate the authenticity of a website. Each method provides a different level of security for consumers.</p></p>
