<p><p>Dry bulk material handling system market can be divided into two main types, Loading Equipment and Conveying Equipment. Loading Equipment includes devices such as hoppers, loaders, and bucket elevators that are used to load bulk materials onto trucks, ships, or railcars. Conveying Equipment, on the other hand, includes systems like belt conveyors, screw conveyors, and pneumatic conveyors that are used to transport bulk materials within a facility or between different locations. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in the efficient handling and transportation of dry bulk materials.</p></p>
