Middle range sonar systems are suited for mid-sized ships operating in coastal or territorial waters, providing accurate detection within moderate distances. Short distance sonar systems are used by smaller boats or vessels in confined spaces, offering precise object detection at close range for maneuvering and obstacle avoidance.</p></p>
