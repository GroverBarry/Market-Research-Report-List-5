<p><p>Heat treated gear services market can be categorized into different types based on the specific treatment processes they offer. Normalizing heat treatment service involves heating the gear to a certain temperature and then allowing it to cool in still air. Quenching heat treatment service involves rapid cooling of the gear in a liquid medium to increase its hardness. Tempering heat treatment service is the process of reheating the gear after quenching to improve its toughness. Annealing heat treatment service involves heating the gear to a specific temperature and then slowly cooling it to relieve internal stresses.</p></p>
