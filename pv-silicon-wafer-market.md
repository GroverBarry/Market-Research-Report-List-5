<p><p>The PV silicon wafer market consists of two main types: monocrystalline silicon wafer and polycrystalline silicon wafer. Monocrystalline silicon wafers are made from a single continuous crystal structure, providing higher efficiency and purity, making them more expensive. Polycrystalline silicon wafers are made from multiple silicon crystals, making them less costly but slightly less efficient. Both types of silicon wafers are commonly used in the production of solar panels, with monocrystalline wafers being favored for high-efficiency applications.</p></p>
