<p><p>Apron washers can be classified into two main types - manual and automatic. Manual apron washers require the user to physically operate the equipment by loading and unloading the aprons, as well as controlling the washing process. On the other hand, automatic apron washers are equipped with sensors and programmable controls that allow for automated washing cycles without the need for manual intervention. Both types of apron washers cater to different market needs, with manual washers being more cost-effective and suitable for smaller operations, while automatic washers are ideal for larger scale and more advanced applications.</p></p>
