<p><ul><li>Internal Circulation Ballscrew</li><li>External Circulation Ballscrew</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultra Precision Ball Screw Market Types refer to the different configurations of ball screws based on the circulation of balls within the screw mechanism. Internal Circulation Ball Screws have the balls contained within the nut and screw assembly, leading to smoother operation and high precision. External Circulation Ball Screws have the balls circulating externally between the nut and screw, providing higher load capacities and longer service life. Both types cater to specific applications and industries requiring ultra precision and high performance.</p></p>
