<p><p>Hematology analyzers are used to analyze blood cells and detect diseases such as anemia, leukemia, and blood clotting disorders. There are two main types of hematology analyzers: automatic and semiautomatic. Automatic analyzers require minimal human intervention and can process a large number of samples quickly, making them ideal for high-volume laboratories. Semiautomatic analyzers involve more manual steps and are typically used in smaller labs or for smaller sample sizes. Both types play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring blood-related disorders.</p></p>
