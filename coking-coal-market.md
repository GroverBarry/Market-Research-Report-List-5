<p><p>Coking coal market types include Hard Coking Coals (HCC), which are used for high-strength coke production in blast furnaces; Medium Coking Coal, which has lower fluidity compared to HCC but is still suitable for coke production; Semi-soft Coking Coal (SSCC), which is softer and less dense than HCC and Medium Coking Coal but can still be used in coke production; and Pulverized Coal Injection (PCI) Coal, which is used as a supplement in blast furnaces to reduce the amount of coke needed.</p></p>
