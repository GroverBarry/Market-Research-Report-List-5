<p><p>Galvanometer scanners are used to control the movement of laser beams in various applications. There are three main types of galvanometer scanners on the market: single axis, two-axis, and triaxial scanners. Single-axis scanners move the laser beam along one axis, two-axis scanners move it along two orthogonal axes, and triaxial scanners offer three-dimensional control by adding another axis of movement. Each type of scanner offers different levels of precision and flexibility in controlling the laser beam's path.</p></p>
