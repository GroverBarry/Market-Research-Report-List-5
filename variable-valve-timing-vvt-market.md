<p><p>Variable Valve Timing (VVT) technology is used in internal combustion engines to optimize performance, fuel efficiency, and emissions. Continuous VVT systems continuously adjust valve timing to maximize engine output across different RPM ranges, providing optimal power delivery. On the other hand, non-continuous VVT systems, like on/off VVT, switch between different valve timing profiles for specific operating conditions. Continuous VVT systems offer more precise control and flexibility in engine operation, while non-continuous VVT systems provide simpler and cost-effective solutions for certain applications.</p></p>
