<p><p>Micro-Display Market Types include Reflective Displays and Transmissive Displays. Reflective displays use ambient light to create images, making them energy efficient and ideal for outdoor use. Transmissive displays, on the other hand, use a light source to project images onto the screen, offering better visibility in bright settings. Reflective displays are commonly used in e-readers and smart glasses, while transmissive displays are popular in VR headsets and digital cameras. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
