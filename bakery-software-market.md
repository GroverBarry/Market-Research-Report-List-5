<p><p>Bakery software can be classified into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based bakery software operates on remote servers, allowing users to access their data and applications over the internet. This type of software is often more flexible, scalable, and cost-effective. On the other hand, on-premises bakery software is installed and operated on a local server at the bakery's premises. This type of software offers greater control and security but may require more upfront costs and maintenance. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
