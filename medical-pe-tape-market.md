<p><p>Medical PE Tape Market Types - LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) cater to different needs in the medical industry. LDPE tapes are softer and more flexible, making them ideal for sensitive skin applications such as wound dressing. On the other hand, HDPE tapes are more durable and provide better moisture resistance, making them suitable for securing medical devices or equipment. Both types offer excellent adhesion properties and are widely used in hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities for various medical purposes.</p></p>
