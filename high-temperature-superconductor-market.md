<p><p>The high temperature superconductor (HTS) market is divided into two main types: 1G HTS and 2G HTS. 1G HTS refers to first-generation superconductors, which typically consist of a single layer of superconducting material. Each type has its own advantages and applications, with 2G HTS generally offering higher performance and efficiency compared to 1G HTS.</p></p>
