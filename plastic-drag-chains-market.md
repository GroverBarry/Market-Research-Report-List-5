<p><p>Plastic Drag Chains are utilized in various industries to protect cables and hoses from damage and wear. These chains come in two main types: Open Type and Closed Type. Open Type drag chains have a crossbar design that allows for easy access to cables, making them suitable for applications where frequent maintenance is needed. On the other hand, Closed Type drag chains enclose cables and hoses completely, providing additional protection against debris and fluids. Both types cater to different industry needs and requirements.</p></p>
