<p><p>LED Fluorescent Market Types include Direct Contact, Remote Phosphor, Thin Film, Modular Light Engine, and Package Free. Direct Contact LEDs have the phosphor coating directly on the LED chip, providing high efficiency. Remote Phosphor LEDs separate the phosphor layer from the LED chip, allowing for better light quality. Thin Film LEDs use a thin layer of phosphor on the LED surface. Modular Light Engine LEDs are customizable light sources that can be easily integrated into different fixtures. Package Free LEDs eliminate the traditional packaging, reducing material waste and cost.</p></p>
