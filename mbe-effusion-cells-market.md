<p><p>MBE Effusion Cells are used in molecular beam epitaxy for thin film deposition. They come in three main types based on temperature ranges: Low Temperature Effusion Cells operate under 300°C, usually used for high vapor pressure materials. Medium Temperature Effusion Cells operate between 300-600°C, suitable for a wide range of materials. High Temperature Effusion Cells operate above 600°C, mainly used for high melting point materials. Each type of effusion cell caters to specific material requirements in MBE applications.</p></p>
