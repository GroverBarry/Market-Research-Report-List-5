<p><p>Building sealants are primarily categorized into four types - Silicone, Polyurethane, Polysulfide, and Others. Silicone sealants are known for their flexibility and durability, making them ideal for various construction applications. Polyurethane sealants offer excellent adhesion and resistance to weathering, while Polysulfide sealants are preferred for their chemical resistance. The "Others" category includes sealants like acrylic, butyl rubber, and hybrid sealants. Each type of sealant serves a specific purpose in the building industry, catering to different needs and requirements.</p></p>
