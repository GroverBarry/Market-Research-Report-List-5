<p><p>Universal IO Module market includes different types such as digital and analog modules. Digital modules help in connecting devices that transmit data in binary form (on/off signals), while analog modules facilitate communication between devices that transmit data in a continuous variable form (such as voltage or current). Digital modules are suitable for applications requiring simple on/off control, while analog modules are better for applications requiring precise control and monitoring of variables. Both types of modules are important in various industries for automation and process control.</p></p>
