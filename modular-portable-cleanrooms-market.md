<p><p>Modular portable cleanrooms are available in two main types: softwall cleanrooms and hardwall cleanrooms. Softwall cleanrooms are constructed using flexible vinyl curtains or panels, offering flexibility and easy customization. These are suitable for less stringent cleanroom requirements. On the other hand, hardwall cleanrooms are constructed using rigid walls and ceilings, providing a more robust and controlled environment. These are ideal for applications requiring higher levels of cleanliness and control over air quality. Both types offer modular and portable solutions for various industries.</p></p>
