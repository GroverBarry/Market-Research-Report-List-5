<p><p>Conveyor for sawdust market includes two main types - Single Belt Conveyor and Twin Belt Conveyor. Single Belt Conveyor is designed with a single belt moving sawdust from one point to another efficiently. On the other hand, Twin Belt Conveyor consists of two belts working in sync to handle larger volumes of sawdust and provide higher throughput. Both types offer solutions for transporting sawdust in woodworking industries, but Twin Belt Conveyor is preferred for heavy-duty applications with larger quantities of material to be moved.</p></p>
