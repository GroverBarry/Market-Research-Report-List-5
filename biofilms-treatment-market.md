<p><p>Biofilms are communities of microorganisms that adhere to surfaces and form a protective extracellular matrix, making them resistant to antimicrobial agents. Biofilms are responsible for various infections and chronic diseases in humans, such as dental plaque, urinary tract infections, and chronic wounds. Biofilms treatment involves targeting and disrupting the biofilm matrix to make the microorganisms susceptible to antimicrobial agents.</p><p>The Biofilms Treatment Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period. The increasing prevalence of biofilm-related infections, growing geriatric population, and the rise in surgical procedures are expected to drive market growth. The development of advanced technologies for biofilm detection and treatment, along with the increasing focus on research and development activities, are also contributing to market expansion.</p><p>The market is witnessing a shift towards the development of novel therapies and products for biofilms treatment, as traditional antimicrobial agents are becoming less effective due to biofilm resistance.
