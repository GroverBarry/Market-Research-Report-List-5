<p><p>The data center busbars market can be categorized into three main types - 3 Phase 4 Wire, 3 Phase 5 Wire, and Others. 3 Phase 4 Wire busbars are used for distributing power in three-phase systems with a neutral wire. 3 Phase 5 Wire busbars are used when a separate ground or earth wire is required in addition to the three phase wires. The category of Others includes busbars designed for specific applications or configurations outside of the standard 3 Phase 4 Wire and 3 Phase 5 Wire setups.</p></p>
