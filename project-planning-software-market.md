<p><p>Project planning software is available in two main market types – on-premises and cloud-based. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted online by the software provider and accessed through the internet. Cloud-based software offers greater flexibility, scalability, and accessibility, making it popular among businesses of all sizes. On-premises software, on the other hand, offers more control over data and security for organizations with specific compliance requirements.</p></p>
