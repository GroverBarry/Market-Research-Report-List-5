<p><ul><li>SmCo5</li><li>Sm2Co17</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Samarium-Cobalt magnets are categorized into two main types, SmCo5 and Sm2Co17. SmCo5 magnets have a higher energy product but lower coercivity, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. On the other hand, Sm2Co17 magnets have higher coercivity and are more resistant to demagnetization, making them ideal for high-performance motors and magnetic devices. Both types of magnets are known for their excellent thermal stability and corrosion resistance, making them popular choices in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
