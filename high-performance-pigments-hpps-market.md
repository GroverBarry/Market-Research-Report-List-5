<p><p>High-performance pigments (HPPs) are advanced colorants known for their superior color strength, durability, and lightfastness compared to traditional pigments. They are categorized as organic and inorganic HPPs. Organic HPPs, derived from carbon-based compounds, offer vibrant hues and excellent solubility, making them ideal for applications in plastics, coatings, and cosmetics. Inorganic HPPs, comprised of minerals and metals, provide exceptional heat and light resistance, making them suitable for use in high-temperature coatings, automotive paints, and specialty inks.</p></p>
