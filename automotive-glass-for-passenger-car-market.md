<p><p>Automotive glass for passenger cars includes various types such as tempered glass, laminated glass, and other specialty glasses. Tempered glass is strengthened through a heating and rapid cooling process, making it more durable and shatter-resistant. Laminated glass consists of two or more layers of glass with a PVB interlayer, providing improved safety by holding the glass together upon impact. Other specialty glasses may include acoustic glass for noise reduction or heated glass for defogging. These different types cater to various safety, durability, and performance needs in the automotive industry.</p></p>
