<p><p>Telematics in the automotive market refers to the technology that allows for the transmission of data between a vehicle and a remote server. There are three main types of telematics systems: embedded, integrated, and tethered. Embedded systems are built directly into the vehicle's hardware, while integrated systems use a smartphone or mobile device to connect. Tethered systems rely on external devices for connectivity. Each type offers different levels of functionality and convenience for users, catering to various preferences and needs.</p></p>
