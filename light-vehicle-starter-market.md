<p><strong>The Light Vehicle Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Light Vehicle Starter market consists of two main types: Direct Control and Electromagnetic Control. Direct Control starters use a solenoid to engage the starter motor directly, providing a quick and efficient start to the vehicle. On the other hand, Electromagnetic Control starters use an electromagnetic switch to engage the starter motor, offering a smoother and more controlled start. Both types have their own advantages and are commonly used in light vehicle applications for reliable starting performance.</p></p>
