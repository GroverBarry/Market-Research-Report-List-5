<p><p>Vehicle security systems market consists of various types such as alarm systems, immobilizers, remote keyless entry systems, passive keyless entry systems, and central locking systems. Alarm systems alert the owner of unauthorized access, immobilizers prevent the vehicle from starting without the correct key, while remote keyless entry allows for wireless locking and unlocking. Passive keyless entry enables hands-free access, and central locking systems secure all doors simultaneously. These systems provide different levels of protection for vehicles from theft and unauthorized entry.</p></p>
