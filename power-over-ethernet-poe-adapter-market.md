<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) adapter market is divided into two main types: client side and server side. Client side PoE adapters are used to power devices such as IP cameras, VoIP phones, and access points directly from the network switch. On the other hand, server side PoE adapters are used to power network switches, routers, and other networking equipment. Both types of PoE adapters provide a convenient and cost-effective solution for powering devices over Ethernet cables, eliminating the need for separate power sources.</p></p>
