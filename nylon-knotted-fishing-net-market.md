<p><ul><li>Depthway</li><li>Lengthway</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nylon knotted fishing nets are commonly classified into two market types based on the way they are constructed: Depthway and Lengthway nets. Depthway nets are constructed with knots running perpendicular to the length of the net, providing high durability and strength for deepwater fishing. On the other hand, Lengthway nets have knots running parallel to the length of the net, making them ideal for capturing fish close to the surface or for use in shallower waters. Both market types offer unique advantages depending on the fishing requirements.</p></p>
