<p><ul><li>Microporous Materials (Less than 2 nm)</li><li>Mesoporous Materials (2-50 nm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microporous materials have pore sizes less than 2 nm, which make them suitable for gas separation and storage applications. These materials offer high surface areas and selective adsorption properties due to their small pores. On the other hand, mesoporous materials have pore sizes ranging from 2-50 nm, making them suitable for catalysis, drug delivery, and sensing applications. Their larger pores allow for higher diffusion rates and easier access for molecules. Both types of materials play a crucial role in various industries due to their unique pore structures and properties.</p></p>
