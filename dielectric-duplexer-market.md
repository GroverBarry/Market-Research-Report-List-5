<p><p>The Dielectric Duplexer market can be classified into two main types: Mono Type and Array Type. Mono Type dielectric duplexers consist of a single cavity and are used for simple applications where there is limited space available. On the other hand, Array Type duplexers consist of multiple cavities arranged in an array, providing better performance and versatility for more complex communication systems. Both types cater to different market needs and requirements in terms of size, performance, and functionality.</p></p>
