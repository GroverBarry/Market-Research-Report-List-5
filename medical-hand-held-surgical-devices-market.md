<p><p>The medical hand-held surgical devices market encompasses various types of instruments used during surgical procedures. Forceps are used for grasping and holding tissues, while retractors are used to hold back organs or tissues during surgery. Dilators are used to expand or enlarge an opening in the body. Graspers are used for holding and manipulating tissues. Scalpels are precise cutting instruments. Other hand-held surgical devices include dissectors, scissors, and bone instruments. These tools play a crucial role in ensuring successful surgical outcomes.</p></p>
