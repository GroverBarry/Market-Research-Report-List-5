<p><strong>The Refractories Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaped Refractories</li><li>Unshaped Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refractories market can be categorized into two main types: Shaped and Unshaped refractories. Shaped refractories are precast shapes like bricks or tiles, which are used for lining furnaces and other high-temperature applications. On the other hand, unshaped refractories are monolithic materials like castables, plastics, and ramming mixes, which are applied in a molten state and then harden at high temperatures. Both types play a crucial role in providing thermal insulation and resistance to heat in various industrial processes.</p></p>
