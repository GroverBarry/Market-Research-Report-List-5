<p><p>Automotive heat treatment furnaces come in two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical furnaces are designed to heat treat long or tall parts vertically, while horizontal furnaces are ideal for treating flat or bulkier parts horizontally. Vertical furnaces offer better uniformity of heat distribution due to the natural convection flow, making them suitable for delicate or precise components. On the other hand, horizontal furnaces are more versatile and can accommodate a wider range of part sizes and shapes, making them a popular choice for many automotive manufacturers.</p></p>
