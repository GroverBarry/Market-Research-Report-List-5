<p><p>Functional feed additives are divided into three main types: sweeteners, fragrances, and acidity regulators. Sweeteners are used to improve palatability of feed and enhance animal consumption. Fragrances can mask unappealing odors in feed and encourage animals to eat more. Acidity regulators help maintain proper pH levels in the digestive system, improving nutrient absorption. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring animals receive the necessary nutrients from their feed, ultimately leading to better overall health and performance.</p></p>
