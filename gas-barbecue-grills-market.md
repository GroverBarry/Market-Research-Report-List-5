LP barbecue grills use propane tanks as fuel, providing a versatile and portable option for outdoor cooking. On the other hand, NG barbecue grills are connected to a natural gas line, offering a more convenient and cost-effective solution for consistent grilling. Both LP and NG grills offer reliable heat control and are popular choices for outdoor cooking enthusiasts looking for efficient and easy-to-use options.</p></p>
