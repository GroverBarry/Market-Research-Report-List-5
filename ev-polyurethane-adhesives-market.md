<p><p>EV Polyurethane adhesives market is segmented into two main types: water-based and solvent-based polyurethane adhesives. Water-based polyurethane adhesives are environmentally friendly and have low VOC emissions, making them suitable for indoor applications. Solvent-based polyurethane adhesives, on the other hand, offer higher bonding strength and resistance to harsh weather conditions, making them ideal for outdoor applications. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the project and the desired properties of the adhesive.</p></p>
