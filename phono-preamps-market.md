<p><ul><li>Moving Magnet (MM) Phono Preamps</li><li>Moving Coil (MC) Phono Preamps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phono preamps come in various types to accommodate different types of turntable cartridges. Moving Magnet (MM) Phono Preamps are designed for the most common type of cartridge and provide the necessary amplification for this type of cartridge. Moving Coil (MC) Phono Preamps are specifically designed for the less common but higher quality moving coil cartridges, which require a different type of amplification. Both types of phono preamps cater to different needs and preferences of audiophiles and turntable enthusiasts.</p></p>
