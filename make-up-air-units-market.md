<p><p>Make-up Air Units are categorized into three main types based on their heating sources: Direct Gas Supplement, Indirect Gas Supplement, and Non-gas Supplement market. Direct Gas Supplement units utilize gas as the primary heat source to warm the air entering a building. Indirect Gas Supplement units heat the air indirectly using a heat exchanger. Non-gas Supplement units do not rely on gas for heating and instead utilize alternative heat sources such as electricity or steam. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications.</p></p>
