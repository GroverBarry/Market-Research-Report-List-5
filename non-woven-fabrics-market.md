<p><p>Non-woven fabrics market consists of three main types - Dry-Laid Type, Spunmelt Type, and Wet-Laid Type. Dry-Laid Type involves the use of mechanical or air-laid processes to bond fibers together. Spunmelt Type is produced by melting and extruding fibers onto a conveyor belt to create a fabric. Wet-Laid Type involves a wet process where fibers are suspended in a liquid and then formed into a fabric. Each type has its own unique characteristics and applications within the non-woven fabrics industry.</p></p>
