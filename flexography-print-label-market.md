<p><p>Flexography print labels are used in various market segments such as pressure sensitive labels, glue-applied labels, heat-shrink and stretch sleeve labels, and in-mold labels. Pressure sensitive labels adhere to surfaces upon application of pressure. Glue-applied labels require adhesives for attachment. Heat-shrink and stretch sleeve labels shrink to fit the packaging upon heat application. In-mold labels are fused with packaging during the manufacturing process. Each type caters to different packaging and labeling requirements in diverse industries.</p></p>
