<p><strong>The Organic Elemental Analyzer (OEA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GC Chromatography</li><li>Frontal Chromatography</li><li>Adsorption-Desorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Organic Elemental Analyzer (OEA) market includes different types such as GC Chromatography, Frontal Chromatography, and Adsorption-Desorption. GC Chromatography involves separating compounds in a mixture based on their different affinities for a stationary phase. Frontal Chromatography refers to a method where a sample continuously passes through a column and the separation occurs based on the dynamic adsorption/desorption process. Adsorption-Desorption involves the adsorption of components onto a solid surface followed by desorption for analysis. Each type offers unique benefits for conducting organic elemental analysis.</p></p>
