Optical analyzers utilize light to measure gases in the sample. Infrared analyzers use infrared light to identify gases. Multiple Gas Analyzers are capable of measuring multiple gases simultaneously, providing a comprehensive analysis of gas mixtures in medical environments.</p></p>
