<p><p>CNC workholding products can be categorized into different types such as Clamping Vise, Work Supports, Workholding Cylinders, Chucks, and Others. Clamping vises are used to securely hold the workpiece in place during machining operations. Work supports provide additional stability and support to the workpiece. Workholding cylinders use hydraulic or pneumatic pressure to clamp the workpiece. Chucks are used to hold rotating workpieces. Other types of workholding products include fixtures, collets, and rotary tables. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring precise and efficient machining processes.</p></p>
