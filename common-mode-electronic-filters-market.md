<p><strong>The Common Mode Electronic Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-pass</li><li>Low-pass</li><li>Band-pass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Common mode electronic filters are used to suppress electromagnetic interference. High-pass filters allow high frequency signals to pass through while attenuating low frequency signals. Low-pass filters only allow low frequency signals to pass through, blocking high frequency signals. Band-pass filters allow a specific range of frequencies to pass through while attenuating signals outside of that range. Each type of filter has specific applications based on the frequencies of interference that need to be suppressed.</p></p>
