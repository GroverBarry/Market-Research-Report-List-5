<p><p>Integrated Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) technology is used for wastewater treatment by combining biological processes with membrane filtration. There are three main types of membranes used in MBR systems: Hollow Fiber Membrane, Flat Sheet Membrane, and Multi-Tubular Membrane. </p><p>Hollow fiber membranes have a high surface area-to-volume ratio, flat sheet membranes offer easy cleaning and maintenance, and multi-tubular membranes provide high permeability. Each type of membrane caters to different treatment needs and applications in the wastewater treatment industry.</p></p>
