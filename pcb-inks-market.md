<p><p>PCB inks are used in printed circuit boards for various applications. There are two main types of PCB inks: Thermal Cure and UV Cure. Thermal Cure inks require heat to cure and harden, providing a durable finish. UV Cure inks, on the other hand, can cure quickly under ultraviolet light, making them ideal for fast production processes. Both types offer different benefits and are widely used in the electronics industry for manufacturing printed circuit boards with different requirements.</p></p>
