<p><p>Single use technology welded metal bellows market includes two main types: edge welded bellows and formed welded bellows. Edge welded bellows are produced by welding a thin strip of metal around the perimeter of each convoluted section, providing flexibility and reliability in demanding applications. Formed welded bellows are produced by forming a seamless tube into a convoluted shape and then welding the ends together, offering high performance in critical applications requiring precision and durability.</p></p>
