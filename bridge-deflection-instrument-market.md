<p><p>Dynamic Bridge Deflection Instrument measures real-time deflection of a bridge under varying loads such as traffic or weather conditions. It provides immediate data on the bridge's structural integrity and performance. Static Bridge Deflection Instrument, on the other hand, measures deflection under constant loads to assess long-term structural behavior and stability. Both types of instruments play a crucial role in monitoring and maintaining the safety and functionality of bridges, ensuring their longevity and preventing potential hazards.</p></p>
