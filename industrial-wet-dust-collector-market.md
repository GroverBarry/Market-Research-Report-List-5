<p><ul><li>Central Wet Dust Collector</li><li>DownDraft Table Dust Collector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Central Wet Dust Collectors are large, centralized systems designed to capture and remove dust and particles from the air in industrial settings. They are typically connected to multiple workstations or machines through a network of ducts. DownDraft Table Dust Collectors, on the other hand, are smaller, portable units that are directly integrated into workbenches or tables. They create a downward airflow to capture dust and debris at the source. Both types of dust collectors are essential for maintaining a clean and safe working environment in industries.</p></p>
