<p><p>The Mobile Continuous Glucose Monitoring System market consists of two types: Invasive and Non-invasive (Exfoliation). Invasive devices require the insertion of a sensor under the skin to continuously monitor glucose levels, providing accurate real-time data for diabetic patients. On the other hand, Non-invasive devices use exfoliation technology to measure glucose levels through the skin without the need for puncturing. This type offers a more convenient and painless option for monitoring glucose levels, making it a popular choice among users.</p></p>
