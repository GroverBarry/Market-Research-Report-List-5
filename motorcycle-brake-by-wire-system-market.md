<p><p>A single-channel type motorcycle brake-by-wire system refers to a system where one control unit regulates the braking force for both the front and rear brakes. On the other hand, a multiple-channel type system consists of separate control units for the front and rear brakes, allowing for independent control and optimization of braking force distribution. Both types offer benefits such as enhanced safety, improved braking performance, and increased rider control, catering to different preferences and requirements in the motorcycle market.</p></p>
