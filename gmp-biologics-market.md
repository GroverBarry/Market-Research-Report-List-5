<p><p>GMP Biologics market consists of Monoclonal Antibodies and Polyclonal Antibodies. Monoclonal Antibodies are designed to target a specific antigen with high specificity, making them ideal for precision medicine. Polyclonal Antibodies, on the other hand, are derived from a mixture of antibodies produced by different B cells and are used for a broader range of targets. Both types of antibodies are essential in the field of biologics for therapeutic and diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
