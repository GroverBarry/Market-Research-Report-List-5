<p><p>Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) regulators come in two main types: Direct Acting Type and Spring-Loaded Type. Direct Acting Type regulators operate based on the changing pressure in the system and adjust the flow of gas accordingly. Spring-Loaded Type regulators, on the other hand, use a spring mechanism to control the flow of gas. Both types are commonly used in LPG systems to ensure safe and efficient gas flow for various applications.</p></p>
