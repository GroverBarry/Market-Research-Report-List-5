<p><p>Optical projection screens are available in different types, with two popular options being Fresnel screens and Black Grid screens. Fresnel screens are designed with a series of concentric grooves on the surface to enhance brightness and image clarity. On the other hand, Black Grid screens feature a grid pattern with black bars that help improve contrast and reduce glare. Both types of screens are commonly used in various settings, such as classrooms, boardrooms, and home theaters, to provide a high-quality viewing experience for presentations or entertainment.</p></p>
