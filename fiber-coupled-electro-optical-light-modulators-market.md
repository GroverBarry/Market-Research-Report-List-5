<p><p>Fiber-coupled electro-optical light modulators are devices that control the intensity or phase of light passing through optical fibers. Electro-optical intensity modulators adjust the intensity of light by varying the voltage applied to the device, making them suitable for applications like telecommunications and sensing. Electro-optical phase modulators, on the other hand, change the phase of light waves by altering the refractive index of the material, enabling applications such as optical signal processing and interferometry. Both types of modulators play a crucial role in modern optical systems.</p></p>
