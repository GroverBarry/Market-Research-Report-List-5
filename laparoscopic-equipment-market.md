<p><p>The laparoscopic equipment market can be segmented into various types including energy systems, hand access instruments, trocars, closure devices, insufflation devices, suction devices, and others. Trocars are used to create a path for inserting surgical instruments. Closure devices help secure incisions after surgery. Insufflation devices maintain pneumoperitoneum. Suction devices remove fluids and debris from the surgical site. Other accessories include specimen retrieval bags and clip appliers.</p></p>
