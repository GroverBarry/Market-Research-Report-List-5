<p><p>Vaginal panel testing market includes two main types of tests - direct molecular DNA probe tests and molecular nucleic acid amplification tests (NAAT). Direct molecular DNA probe tests involve identifying specific DNA sequences of pathogens, while NAAT tests involve amplifying and detecting specific nucleic acid sequences of pathogens. These tests are used to diagnose various vaginal infections such as bacterial vaginosis, yeast infections, and sexually transmitted infections. Both types of tests provide rapid and accurate results, helping healthcare providers make timely treatment decisions.</p></p>
