<p><p>Natural resin products can be broadly categorized into two main types: vegetable resin and animal resin. Vegetable resin, also known as plant resin, is derived from various plants such as pine trees and is commonly used in the production of varnishes and adhesives. On the other hand, animal resin, such as amber and shellac, is produced by certain insects or animals and is used in jewelry making and as a coating for wood products. Both types of resin products have unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
