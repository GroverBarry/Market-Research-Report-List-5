<p><p>Skid steer loaders come in two main types: wheeled and tracked. Wheeled skid steer loaders are best suited for working on smooth, hard surfaces like concrete or pavement. They offer better maneuverability and a higher travel speed compared to tracked skid steers. Tracked skid steer loaders, on the other hand, are ideal for rough or soft terrain like mud, gravel, or snow. They provide better traction and stability in challenging conditions, making them suitable for construction sites or agricultural applications.</p></p>
