<p><p>Underfloor heating control systems are available in two main types: electric and hydraulic. Electric systems utilize electric wires or heating mats installed beneath the floor to generate heat. They are highly efficient and easy to install. On the other hand, hydraulic systems use pipes filled with hot water to provide warmth. They are more commonly used in large-scale projects and are known for their durability and consistent heat distribution. Both types offer a range of control options to adjust the temperature and ensure efficient heating throughout the space.</p></p>
