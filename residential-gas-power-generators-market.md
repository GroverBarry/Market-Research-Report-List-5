<p><p>Residential gas power generators come in two main types: portable generators and standby generators. Portable generators are smaller, more versatile, and can be easily moved around to provide temporary power during outages or when off the grid. Standby generators, on the other hand, are larger, more powerful, and are permanently installed outside the home. They automatically kick in during power outages to provide continuous, uninterrupted power to the entire household. Both types serve as crucial backup power sources for residential properties.</p></p>
