<p><p>Automated Endoscope Reprocessors (AERs) are medical devices used to disinfect and clean endoscopes between uses. There are two main types of AERs on the market: Single Chamber and Multi Chamber. Single Chamber AERs are designed to process one endoscope at a time, while Multi Chamber AERs can process multiple endoscopes simultaneously in separate chambers. Single Chamber AERs are typically more compact and cost-effective, while Multi Chamber AERs are more efficient for facilities with high endoscope cleaning needs.</p></p>
