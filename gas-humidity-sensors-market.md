<p><p>Gas humidity sensors can be classified into two main categories: digital and analog. Digital gas humidity sensors utilize digital outputs to provide accurate and precise humidity measurements. They are commonly used in applications where precise control and monitoring of humidity levels are required. On the other hand, analog gas humidity sensors provide continuous output signals that vary linearly with changes in humidity levels. They are often preferred in applications where real-time monitoring of humidity levels is essential. Both digital and analog gas humidity sensors have their own unique advantages and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
