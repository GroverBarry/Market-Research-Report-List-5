<p><p>Asphalt paving fabric is classified into two main types: Woven Fibres and Non-Woven Fibres. Woven Fibres are made by weaving together threads to create a strong and durable fabric that is used to reinforce asphalt pavement. Non-Woven Fibres are created by bonding fibers together without weaving, providing a cost-effective solution for enhancing the performance and longevity of asphalt surfaces. Both types of paving fabric offer benefits such as improved tensile strength, reduced cracking, and enhanced overall durability of asphalt pavements.</p></p>
