<p><strong>The Plant Phenol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lignin Source</li><li>Cellulose Source</li><li>Hemicellulose/Xylan Source</li><li>Other Source</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plant phenols are classified based on their sources within the plant. Lignin source phenols are derived from the protective layer of cells in plant tissues, providing structural support and rigidity. Cellulose source phenols come from the plant cell walls, which are composed of cellulose fibers. Hemicellulose/Xylan source phenols are extracted from the polysaccharide components of cell walls, and Other Source phenols come from various other parts of the plant. Each source offers unique properties and uses in different industries, contributing to the diverse plant phenol market.</p></p>
