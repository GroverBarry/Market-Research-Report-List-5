<p><p>Side channel blowers are primarily classified into two types: Single Stage Side Channel Blowers and Multistage Side Channel Blowers. Single Stage Side Channel Blowers have a simple design with one impeller, making them suitable for applications requiring low to medium pressure. On the other hand, Multistage Side Channel Blowers have multiple impellers, allowing them to generate higher pressures and airflows. These blowers are commonly used in industries such as wastewater treatment, pneumatic conveying, and vacuum packaging.</p></p>
