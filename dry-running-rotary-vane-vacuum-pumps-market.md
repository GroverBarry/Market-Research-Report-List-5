<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Two Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry running rotary vane vacuum pumps are classified into two main types: Single Stage and Two Stage. Single Stage pumps consist of one rotor and one stator, providing a maximum vacuum level of around 15-28 inHg. On the other hand, Two Stage pumps have two rotors and two stators, achieving a higher maximum vacuum level of around 29-29.9 inHg. Single Stage pumps are suitable for applications where a lower vacuum level is required, while Two Stage pumps are ideal for processes needing a higher vacuum level.</p></p>
