<p><p>Plastic Antenna Element Market Types - LDS-LCP and Modified PPS are two popular materials used for antenna elements in the market. LDS-LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer) is known for its high performing characteristics which include low dielectric losses, excellent thermal stability, and high frequency capabilities. On the other hand, Modified PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide) offers great mechanical properties, chemical resistance, and thermal stability. Both materials are widely used in various industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and telecommunications for their unique advantages in antenna element applications.</p></p>
