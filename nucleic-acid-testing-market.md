<p><ul><li>Transcription-mediated Amplification (TMA)</li><li>Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)</li><li>Ligase Chain Reaction (LCR)</li><li>Whole Genome Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nucleic Acid Testing (NAT) market includes various types of testing methods such as Transcription-mediated Amplification (TMA), Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR), Ligase Chain Reaction (LCR), and Whole Genome Sequencing. TMA amplifies RNA molecules for detection, PCR amplifies DNA sequences, LCR attaches DNA strands using enzymes, and Whole Genome Sequencing analyzes an organism's entire DNA sequence. Each method has its own advantages and applications in diagnosing genetic diseases, infectious diseases, and monitoring treatment effectiveness. These tests play a crucial role in precision medicine and personalized healthcare.</p></p>
