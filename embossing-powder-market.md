<p><p>Embossing powder market can be classified into two main types based on appearance: Transparent type and Opaque type. Transparent type embossing powders are clear when melted, allowing the color of the underlying material to show through. On the other hand, Opaque type embossing powders create a solid, colored finish when melted, covering up the color of the material underneath. Both types offer unique effects and can be used for various crafting and artistic projects.</p></p>
