<p><p>Automotive exhaust system gaskets can be classified into three main types: metal gaskets, non-metal gaskets, and composite gaskets. Metal gaskets are made from materials like stainless steel and are known for their durability and heat resistance. Non-metal gaskets are typically made from materials like rubber or graphite and are more flexible but may not be as heat resistant. Composite gaskets combine the properties of both metal and non-metal materials, offering a balance between durability and flexibility in automotive exhaust systems.</p></p>
