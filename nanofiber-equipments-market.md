<p><p>Nanofiber equipments market is divided into two main types: Melt Blown Machines and Electrospinning Equipment. Melt Blown Machines are used to produce fine fibers by melting thermoplastic materials and blowing them into a stream of high-velocity air. Electrospinning Equipment operates by applying a high voltage to a polymer solution to create nanofibers. Both types of equipment are crucial in the production of nanofibers for various industries such as filtration, healthcare, and electronics.</p></p>
