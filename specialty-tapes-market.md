<p><p>Specialty tapes market includes woven tapes and non-woven tapes. Woven tapes are made by weaving yarns together, offering strong and durable adhesion for various industrial applications. On the other hand, non-woven tapes are created by entangling fibers using heat, chemicals, or mechanical processes, providing flexibility and conformability. Both types of tapes cater to specific needs in industries such as automotive, construction, medical, and packaging, offering unique properties and benefits for different applications.</p></p>
