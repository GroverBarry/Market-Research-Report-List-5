<p><ul><li>Vacuum Toilet System</li><li>Pressured Water Flushing Toilet System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rail vehicle sanitary system market includes two main types: vacuum toilet system and pressured water flushing toilet system. Vacuum toilet systems use suction to remove waste from the toilet bowl, while pressured water flushing toilet systems use water under pressure to flush waste away. These systems provide efficient and hygienic sanitation solutions for passengers on trains and other rail vehicles. The choice between the two types often depends on factors such as space constraints and desired level of water conservation.</p></p>
