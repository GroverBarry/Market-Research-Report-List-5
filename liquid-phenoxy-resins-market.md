<p><p>Liquid Phenoxy Resins are divided into two main markets based on their dispersing medium: Waterborne Phenoxy Resins and Solventborne Phenoxy Resins. Waterborne Phenoxy Resins are dispersed in water, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. They are commonly used in applications where low VOC emissions are required. On the other hand, Solventborne Phenoxy Resins are dispersed in organic solvents, making them ideal for applications requiring high performance and excellent chemical resistance. Both markets cater to a wide range of industries such as coatings, adhesives, and composites.</p></p>
