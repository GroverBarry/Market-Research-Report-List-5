All-Steel tires are made with a steel belt underneath the tread for added strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty vehicles and long-distance driving. On the other hand, Semi-Steel tires have a combination of steel belts and fabric for improved handling and fuel efficiency, making them a popular choice for mid-range vehicles. Both types offer eco-friendly features and contribute to reducing carbon emissions.</p></p>
