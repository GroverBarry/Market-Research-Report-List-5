<p><p>Tire testing services encompass a variety of market types to ensure the quality and performance of tires. Tensile tests evaluate the strength of tire materials under tension, while tear tests assess the resistance to tearing. Adhesion tests measure the bonding strength between tire components. Compression tests evaluate the tire's resistance to external pressure. Fatigue tests simulate repeated stress on the tire to determine its durability over time. These different testing methods are essential for manufacturers to guarantee the safety and reliability of their tires.</p></p>
