<p><p>A chest tube drainage system is used to remove air, fluid, or pus from the pleural cavity. There are two main types of chest tube drainage systems: Single Chamber and Multiple Chamber. In a Single Chamber system, all the drainage collects in one chamber. In a Multiple Chamber system, the drainage is separated into different chambers, allowing for better control and monitoring of the drainage. Both types of systems play a crucial role in managing conditions such as pleural effusion and pneumothorax.</p></p>
