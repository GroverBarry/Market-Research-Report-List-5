<p><p>Silicon wafers are essential components in solar panel manufacturing. There are two main types of silicon wafers in the market - single crystal silicon wafer and polycrystalline silicon wafer. Single crystal wafers are made from a single continuous crystal structure, resulting in higher efficiency but also higher cost. Polycrystalline wafers, on the other hand, are made from multiple smaller crystals, making them more cost-effective but slightly less efficient. Both types play a crucial role in the production of solar panels for renewable energy generation.</p></p>
