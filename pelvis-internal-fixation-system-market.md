<p><p>The Pelvis Internal Fixation System market includes two main types: Locking and Non-Locking systems. Locking systems utilize screws that lock into the plate, providing more stability and reliability in fixation. Non-Locking systems, on the other hand, use conventional screws that do not lock into the plate. Locking systems are often preferred for complex fractures or cases where greater stability is needed, while non-locking systems may be suitable for simpler fractures or situations where flexibility in fixation is desired.</p></p>
