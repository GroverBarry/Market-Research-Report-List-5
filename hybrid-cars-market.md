<p><ul><li>Series Hybrid</li><li>Parallel Hybrid</li><li>Plug-in Hybrid</li><li>Series-Parallel Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are four main market types for hybrid cars: Series Hybrid, Parallel Hybrid, Plug-in Hybrid, and Series-Parallel Hybrid. Series Hybrid vehicles only use the electric motor to propel the car, with a gasoline engine used to generate power for the battery. Parallel Hybrid cars use both the electric motor and gasoline engine to propel the vehicle. Plug-in Hybrid cars can be charged from an external power source in addition to using gasoline. Series-Parallel Hybrid cars combine elements of both Series and Parallel Hybrid systems for optimal efficiency.</p></p>
