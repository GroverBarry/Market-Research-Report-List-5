<p><p>3D Water Cutting Machines are classified into two main types: Sandless Cutting and Abrasive Cutting. Sandless Cutting machines use high-pressure water jets to precisely cut various materials without the need for added abrasives, making them suitable for more delicate materials such as glass and plastics. On the other hand, Abrasive Cutting machines utilize a mixture of water and abrasive materials to cut through harder substances like metal and stone. Each type caters to different market needs and applications based on material hardness and precision requirements.</p></p>
